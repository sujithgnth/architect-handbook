# Company Preparation

Use this section for company-specific interview prep.

For each company, capture:

- role and level;
- product/domain context;
- likely interview rounds;
- core technologies;
- likely system design topics;
- behavioral stories to prepare;
- questions to ask the interviewer.

## Useful Reverse Interview Question

Ask the interviewer to look 12 months ahead and describe what you would need to
achieve for them to say hiring you was a good decision.

This surfaces the real job, not just the job description. Listen for the
business problem, the technical domain, the expected ownership level, and the
first major system or migration they expect the role to influence.
