- Interview notes: `interviews/`

Workflow:

1. Place exercises under the matching topic folder.
2. Include goal, starting constraints, expected behavior, and optional hints.
3. Keep solution notes separate from the prompt when possible.
4. Link back to the relevant concept note.
5. Keep large imported course exports in `Coding Interview/` or ignored
   topic-local `resources/` folders; extract small curated practice prompts
   into `exercises/`.
6. Prefer small tasks that can be completed and reviewed quickly.
7. Run `git diff --check` after edits.
